Account Management

Creation:

You create an account on the /login page. You can do this by inputting a valid email and password and clicking the submit button, or by clicking the google login button and selecting a google account.

Management:

When you login in, you will have a component in the header that contains the options to manage your account. There is a user icon, a button that displays your username which navigates you to your user page and a menu that has 3 options, edit account, delete account and logout. Clicking on edit account will popout an edit account menu. Here you have the option to change your username and user picture. Delete account will popout a confirmation message to make sure you wish to delete your account. logout will take you to the login page and remove any saved auth information.
